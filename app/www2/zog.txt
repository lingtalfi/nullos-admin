
A pane container contains exactly three elements: the main pane, the aux pane, and the pane separator.


- .panes-container
    - .pane-main
    - .pane-separator
    - .pane-aux




You define the split type (horizontal or vertical) by adding an extra class on the pane container:

- .vertical-split: divide the pane container in columns (and applies height:100%)
- .horizontal-split: divide the pane container in rows
